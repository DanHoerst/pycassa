Working With Timelines
======================
Timelines are frequently used in Cassandra when a chronological ordering
of events is important.  These typically use a timestamp or TimeUUID for
column names so that columns within a row are chronologically ordered.
When multiple clients may add items to the timeline, a TimeUUID is
generally used instead of a timestamp to avoid collisions.
